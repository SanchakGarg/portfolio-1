About Us

The Women in Computing Society (WiCS) at Ashoka University is a vibrant community dedicated to empowering and supporting women in the field of technology. Our mission is to create an inclusive environment that fosters growth, learning, and collaboration among students passionate about computing. Through our diverse range of events, workshops, and mentorship programs, we strive to bridge the gender gap in the tech industry and inspire the next generation of women leaders in computing.
